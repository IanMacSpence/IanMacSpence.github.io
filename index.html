Travel to Bali!!!!!!

Bali is an island with lots of things to do and lots of places to see. It belongs to Indonesia and can be found to the north-east of the main islands.

There are many things to do and many places to see in Bali. You could spend the day surfing, snorkeling or hiking. You should definitely take the time to go see a few sites such as Ubud, Canggu and Kuta.

Things to do

  • Surfing
  • Snorkeling
  • Hiking

Places to go

  • Ubud
  • Canggu
  • Kuta
